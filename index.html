    GossipWave

    GossipWave is a social app I designed that centers around trending conversations and topics, offering users a dynamic platform to engage with the latest buzz. The app features real-time discussions, customizable feeds, and interactive elements that keep users connected to what’s hot in the world of social media.     PiggyPal

    Piggypal is a savings app I designed specifically for kids aged 5-10, aimed at teaching them the fundamentals of financial literacy in a fun and engaging way. The app includes features like income and expense tracking, savings goal setting, and interactive financial education content. To keep young users motivated, I incorporated gamification elements and visually appealing graphics that make the learning process enjoyable. Accessibility was also a key focus, with the design adhering to WCAG 2.2 AA standards to ensure it is user-friendly for all children. Piggypal empowers kids to develop smart money habits early on, all within a safe and educational environment.

    Letters.

    Letters. is a social media app I designed that brings back the charm of letter writing in the digital age. Each user's page is like a personal diary, filled with long-form letters that resemble blog posts, complete with beautiful typography and embedded images. The app encourages thoughtful communication by starting each letter with a salutation, creating a personal and intimate experience for both writers and readers. Users can respond to letters with their own, forming a thread-like conversation that feels more meaningful than typical social media interactions. Letters. also features AI-assisted writing tools, helping users craft their messages with creativity and ease. The design emphasizes aesthetics, creating a space where the art of letter writing can thrive in a modern, connected world.
